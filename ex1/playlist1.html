The Magic Whip - Blur


The Magic Whip is Blur’s eighth studio album and was released on April 27, 2015 by Parlophone and Warner Bros. Records. It was their first album in 12 years since Think Tank (2003) and represented the longest time between studio albums in the band’s history .

  • The Magic Whip was well received by critics and was a commercial success.
